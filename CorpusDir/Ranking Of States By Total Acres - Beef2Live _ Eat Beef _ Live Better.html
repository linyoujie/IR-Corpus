Ranking Of States By Total Acres


Alaska has the most acres in the United States followed by Texas and California.


The United States has more than 2.4 billion acres of total area (land and water).


Alaska accounts for more than 17% of the total acres in the United States.  Alaska has more acres than Texas, California & Montana combined.


There are only three states with more than 100 million acres of total area in the United States:  Alaska, Texas & California.
